// 06 — FAQ

Frequently Asked

Got a quick question? We've probably answered it below.

How can I track my order? +
Use the tracking link in your shipment confirmation email, sent automatically after dispatch.
Can I cancel my order? +
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of purchase. After this, it will have entered fulfilment.
Do you offer international shipping? +
Currently, Acme Electronics ships within the UK only.
How long does a refund take? +
Refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving your return. Allow a further 1–3 days for your bank.
